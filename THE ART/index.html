THE ART

WHAT IS CALLED ART?

Art is the expression of creativity, imagination, or skill through visual, auditory, or tangible mediums to evoke emotion, thought, or beauty.

7 TYPES OF ART

The seven different art forms are Architecture, Sculpture, Painting, Literature, Music, Performing Arts, CinemaTogether, these art forms weave the fabric of human creativity, leaving a legacy that transcends time and space, continually evolving while reflecting the richness of human culture.
